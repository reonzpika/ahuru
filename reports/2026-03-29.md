## ⚠️ Keyword Cannibalisation

  - `/collections/diffuser-oil-nz` — 14 impressions, position 1.1, 0 clicks

  - `/blogs/guide/oil-diffuser-recipes-nz` — 11 impressions, position 1.2, 0 clicks
- **Recommendation:** The blog guide is clearly winning — leave it as the primary page. Add `<link rel="canonical">` pointing to the guide URL on the two secondary blog posts (`/best-essential-oils-for-diffusers-elevate-your-aromatherapy-experience` and `/essential-oil-in-diffuser`). This prevents Google from seeing them as duplicates.
