## Executive Summary

The site is declining year-on-year in a way that demands strategic attention: clicks are down 44% over the past 90 days compared to the same period last year, despite impressions growing 22% and average position improving dramatically from 29.9 to 9.1. The core structural problem is a severe CTR collapse — from 1.13% to 0.52% — which means Āhuru is ranking better than ever but converting far fewer impressions into clicks. The single most important action this month is diagnosing and addressing the CTR collapse across the site's highest-impression pages, particularly the essential oils blog post sitting at 34,000 impressions with a CTR likely well below 1%. Watch whether "anxiety rings nz" — which has surged from position 31.5 to 3.6 month-on-month — begins generating clicks in April, as that recovery could meaningfully lift fidget ring revenue.

## 🎯 Strategic Priorities This Month

**Priority 1: Diagnose and fix the site-wide CTR collapse**
- **Insight:** Impressions are up 22% YoY and average position has improved from 29.9 to 9.1, yet clicks have fallen 44%. The CTR has dropped from 1.13% to 0.52%. This is the single biggest drag on organic revenue — Āhuru is earning better rankings but failing to convert that visibility into traffic. The essential oils blog post alone has 34,337 impressions this month but its CTR is approximately 0.53%, suggesting weak title/meta alignment with search intent. "Best essential oils for diffuser" ranks at position 3.1 over 90 days with 33 clicks from what would have been thousands of impressions — SERP features may be absorbing volume, but weak snippet copy compounds the loss.
- **Action:** Audit the top 10 pages by impressions in GSC. For each, check: (1) is the title tag actively compelling and benefit-led, not just keyword-matched? (2) is the meta description written as a call-to-action, not a description? (3) is a Google AI Overview or featured snippet likely eating clicks for this query? Rewrite title tags and meta descriptions for the top 5 highest-impression pages this month.
- **Expected outcome:** A 0.1–0.2 pp CTR improvement across high-impression pages within 6–8 weeks. On 438,000 impressions per 90 days, that is 440–870 additional clicks per quarter — equivalent to recovering a meaningful share of the YoY click loss without needing any new rankings.

---

**Priority 2: Build content targeting ADHD and spinner ring variants**
- **Insight:** "Fidget ring for adhd", "fidget ring adhd nz", and "spinner rings nz" are all showing zero impressions in the 28d watchlist — these are not just unranked, they appear to have no current targeting. These are high-intent, commercially valuable queries that sit within Āhuru's exact product and brand positioning. "Anxiety jewellery nz" has also dropped off entirely (7 clicks last year, none this year). The site is leaving meaningful fidget ring adjacent traffic uncaptured.
- **Action:** Create or substantially expand one piece of content targeting the ADHD angle specifically — a blog post or collection page optimised for "fidget ring for ADHD NZ" and related queries. This content already exists as a brand story (wellness, anxiety, sensory relief) and should require minimal new research. Simultaneously, check whether /collections/fidget-rings-nz has any on-page copy mentioning spinner rings and ADHD — if not, add it.
- **Expected outcome:** Initial impressions for ADHD/spinner variants within 4–6 weeks of publishing; click traffic within 8–12 weeks. This also diversifies the fidget ring traffic base so it is less dependent on the core "fidget ring nz" cluster.

---

**Priority 3: Investigate the "anxiety rings nz" ranking surge before relying on it**
- **Insight:** "Anxiety rings nz" has moved from position 31.5 to 3.6 month-on-month — a 27.9-position improvement — yet it generated zero clicks on 33 impressions. This is a significant ranking movement that may be real consolidation, or it may be volatile (the YoY data shows it was at position 1.0 last year with 17 clicks, then fell to 27.3, and is now recovering). Understanding why it collapsed and whether the current recovery is stable is strategically important because this term and the /collections/anxiety-rings-nz page are directly commercial.
- **Action:** Check the /collections/anxiety-rings-nz page for: (1) page title and meta description quality, (2) whether the page has sufficient product listings and descriptive copy to warrant a stable top-5 ranking, (3) any crawl or indexing issues that may explain the historic instability. Treat the current position 3.6 as provisional until it holds for a second consecutive month.
- **Expected outcome:** Confirmation of whether this recovery is stable. If it holds through April, "anxiety rings nz" should begin generating meaningful clicks and the collection page may benefit from a targeted copy refresh to capitalise on the improved position.

## 📈 Trend Diagnosis

Āhuru is in a growth paradox: the site's technical SEO and content authority have clearly improved — ranked pages are up 66 YoY, average position has moved from 29.9 to 9.1, and several important queries are now in positions 1–5 — but the business outcome (clicks, and therefore revenue opportunity from organic) is declining sharply. The root cause is almost certainly a combination of two factors: SERP feature displacement (AI Overviews and featured snippets absorbing clicks on informational queries where Āhuru ranks well, particularly the essential oils content) and title/meta copy that is not compelling enough to earn clicks even when ranking prominently. Fidget rings have the strongest momentum of any category and the watchlist positions are healthy, but the category is punching below its weight on click yield given its ranking strength. The essential oils content cluster is the most at risk — it drives the majority of impressions but an extremely low CTR, and if SERP features continue to expand for "best essential oils for diffuser" type queries, this traffic will keep compressing regardless of ranking. The single biggest structural issue that one month of focused work could address is CTR optimisation across the top 10 pages by impressions: this does not require new content or new links, just sharper title tags and meta descriptions, and it could recover hundreds of monthly clicks from rankings that already exist.
